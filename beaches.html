Beach Essentials

Sun Protection

Bring sunscreen (SPF 30+), sunglasses, and a hat to protect from Mumbai's strong sun, especially between 10am-4pm.

Swimming Safety

Most Mumbai beaches have strong currents. Swim only in designated safe zones and never alone.

Beach Eats

Try local street food like bhel puri and coconut water, but choose stalls with good hygiene practices.
