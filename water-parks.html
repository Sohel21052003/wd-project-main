Water Park Safety Tips

Life Jackets

Always wear provided life jackets, especially for wave pools and deep water attractions.

Sun Protection

Apply waterproof sunscreen every 2 hours and wear UV-protective swimwear.

Slide Safety

Follow all posted instructions and maintain proper sliding positions on water slides.
