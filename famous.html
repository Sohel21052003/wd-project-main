Must-See

Gateway of India

Apollo Bandar, Colaba Open 24 hours

The Gateway of India is an arch-monument built in the early 20th century to commemorate the visit of King George V and Queen Mary to Bombay. It later became the ceremonial entrance to India for viceroys and governors.

UNESCO Site

Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus

Fort, Mumbai 4:00 AM - 1:00 AM

This historic railway station is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and an outstanding example of Victorian Gothic Revival architecture in India, blended with themes deriving from Indian traditional architecture.

Scenic

Marine Drive

South Mumbai Open 24 hours

Known as the 'Queen's Necklace' because of its curved shape and street lights that resemble a string of pearls at night, Marine Drive is a 3.6-kilometer-long boulevard along the coast of South Mumbai.

Get Directions
Luxury

Taj Mahal Palace Hotel

Apollo Bandar, Colaba Open 24 hours

One of India's most famous and luxurious hotels, the Taj Mahal Palace opened in 1903. It combines Moorish, Oriental, and Florentine architectural styles and has hosted numerous kings, dignitaries, and celebrities.
